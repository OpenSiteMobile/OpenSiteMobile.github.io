Cookies, Session and Local Storage
We use "Cookies", "Session Storage" and "Local Storage" web browser technologies on this site. A cookie, session local store is a piece or snippet of data stored on a site visitor's hard drive via their browser, to help us improve your access to and navigation through our site. They are also used to identify repeat visitors to our site. For instance, when we use a cookie to identify you, you may not have to log in a password more than once, thereby saving time while on our site. Cookies can also enable us to track and target the interests of our users to enhance the experience on our site. Usage of a cookie is in no way linked to any personally identifiable information on our site. Usage of Cookies, Session and Local Storage data collection is always optional on this site and can easily be restricted via settings available in your specific web browser. Please note that opting out of these technologies will not stop the basic function of this site, but may slow down or add repetition to site interactions.

Some of our business partners may use cookie, session and local storage technologies on our site (for example, advertisers). However, we have no access to or control over these cookies or data stores. Please note, these technologies can easily be restricted or data removed from your device via settings available in your specific web browser.
